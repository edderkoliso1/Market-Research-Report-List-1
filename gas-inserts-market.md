<p><strong>The Gas Inserts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vent-Free</li><li>Direct Vent</li><li>Natural Vent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas inserts market is categorized into three main types: Vent-Free, Direct Vent, and Natural Vent. Vent-Free inserts operate without a chimney, utilizing oxygen from the room, which makes installation easy but requires proper ventilation. Direct Vent inserts use a sealed combustion system, drawing air from outside and expelling exhaust through a vent, ensuring efficiency and safety. Natural Vent inserts rely on traditional chimneys, drawing air from inside and venting outside, offering a classic option but may be less efficient than direct vent systems.</p></p>
