<p><ul><li>Full Side Milling Cutter</li><li>Half-Side Milling Cutter</li><li>Stagger-Tooth Side Milling Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The side milling cutter market comprises three main types. Full side milling cutters feature cutting teeth on both sides, enabling efficient removal of material from wider surfaces. Half-side milling cutters have teeth on only one side, making them ideal for more precise, narrower operations. Stagger-tooth side milling cutters have teeth arranged in a staggered pattern, reducing noise and vibration while improving cutting efficiency. Each type serves specific applications in industries like manufacturing and construction, tailored to various machining needs.</p></p>
