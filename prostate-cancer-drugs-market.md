<p><p>The prostate cancer drugs market encompasses several therapeutic approaches. Hormonal therapy aims to reduce testosterone levels to slow cancer growth. Chemotherapy utilizes cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells, often used in advanced stages. Immunotherapy harnesses the body's immune system to recognize and attack cancer cells, enhancing the immune response. Targeted therapy focuses on specific molecular targets within cancer cells, often leading to more effective treatments with fewer side effects. Each type addresses the disease's complexity and varies based on patient needs.</p></p>
