<p><p>The Betel Leaf Essential Oil market features two primary extraction types: steam distillation and cold pressing. Steam distillation involves passing steam through dried betel leaves, extracting essential oils through vaporization, and then condensing the vapor back into liquid form. This method retains aromatic compounds effectively. Cold pressing, alternatively, involves mechanically pressing fresh betel leaves to extract the oil without heat, preserving its natural properties and aroma. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences, impacting quality, aroma, and application in various industries.</p></p>
