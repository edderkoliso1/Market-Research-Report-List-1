<p><p>The aerosol treatment market comprises various delivery devices for respiratory therapies. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) provide pre-measured doses of medication in aerosol form, facilitating quick and portable administration. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) require patients to inhale deeply to disperse medication in powder form, offering ease of use without propellants. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into mist for inhalation, ideal for severe cases. Other devices may include soft mist inhalers and breath-actuated inhalers, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
