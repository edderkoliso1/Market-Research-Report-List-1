<p><p>Calf feeding systems are categorized into manual and automatic types. Manual systems require caregivers to prepare and feed calves individually, offering more control over nutrition and social interaction but demanding significant labor. In contrast, automatic systems utilize technology to dispense feed at scheduled intervals, ensuring consistent nutrition with reduced labor costs. These systems enhance efficiency and can accommodate larger herds, making them increasingly popular in modern dairy operations as they improve productivity and animal welfare.</p></p>
