<p><p>The lithium battery anode graphitization market is categorized into two primary methods: direct and indirect. The direct method involves the conversion of carbon precursors into graphitic material through high-temperature treatment, resulting in better structural integrity and performance. In contrast, the indirect method processes materials separately before combining them, which can afford flexibility in material selection but may compromise some performance characteristics. Both approaches aim to enhance anode efficiency and battery lifespan, catering to the growing energy storage demands.</p></p>
