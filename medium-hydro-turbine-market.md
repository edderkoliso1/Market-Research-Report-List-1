<p><p>The medium hydro turbine market encompasses several types, including Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are versatile and suitable for varying water heads, making them popular in medium-sized plants. Kaplan turbines are ideal for low-head sites, utilizing adjustable blades for efficiency. Pelton turbines excel in high-head applications, leveraging impulse energy from water jets. Other types may include Turgo and cross-flow turbines, each designed for specific hydrological conditions. Together, these turbine types cater to diverse energy generation needs in medium hydroelectric projects.</p></p>
