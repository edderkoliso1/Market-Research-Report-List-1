<p><strong>The Electrocardiography (ECG) Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holter Monitoring</li><li>Event Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrocardiography (ECG) monitoring encompasses various types, including Holter monitoring and event monitoring. Holter monitoring provides continuous ECG recording over 24 to 48 hours, capturing heart rhythms during daily activities to identify abnormalities. In contrast, event monitoring offers a more intermittent approach, where patients activate the device to record their heart activity during specific symptoms, usually over several weeks. Both methods are crucial for diagnosing cardiac conditions, enabling healthcare providers to monitor patients effectively and determine appropriate treatment.</p></p>
