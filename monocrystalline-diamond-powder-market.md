<p><p>The monocrystalline diamond powder market is categorized into two main types: metal-bonded catalyst single crystal diamond micropowder and resin bond single crystal diamond micron powder. Metal-bonded micropowder is used for applications requiring high durability and wear resistance, typically in industrial cutting and grinding tools. Conversely, resin bond micron powder is favored for precision applications and offers flexibility in formulation, making it suitable for polishing and finishing processes. Both types cater to diverse sectors like manufacturing, electronics, and aerospace.</p></p>
