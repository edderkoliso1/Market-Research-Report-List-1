<p><p>The 3D image sensor market comprises primarily three types: CMOS, CCD, and others. CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption, integration capabilities, and fast processing speeds, making them popular in mobile devices and automotive applications. CCD sensors, while offering superior image quality and low noise levels, generally consume more power and are often used in professional imaging and scientific applications. Other types include hybrid sensors and specialized systems that cater to niche markets, enhancing versatility and performance across various fields.</p></p>
