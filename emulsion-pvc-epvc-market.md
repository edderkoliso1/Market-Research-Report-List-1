<p><p>Emulsion PVC (EPVC) is categorized into two primary market types: homogeneous and heterogeneous. Homogeneous EPVC consists of uniformly mixed polymer particles, providing consistent properties and applications in coatings and adhesives. In contrast, heterogeneous EPVC features distinct phases or regions, resulting in varied properties that suit applications like flexible films and automotive parts. Both types cater to diverse industries based on their unique characteristics, enabling tailored solutions for specific performance requirements and applications across multiple sectors.</p></p>
