<p><p>The probiotic ingredients for animal use market is primarily categorized into three types: bacteria, yeast, and spore formers. Bacteria, such as Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium, promote gut health and enhance digestion. Yeast, like Saccharomyces cerevisiae, supports nutrient absorption and helps in maintaining a balanced microbiome. Spore formers, including Bacillus species, offer stability and resilience, surviving harsh conditions in the digestive tract. Together, these ingredients improve animal health, growth performance, and overall well-being in livestock and pets.</p></p>
