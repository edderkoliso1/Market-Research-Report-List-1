HEPA filters capture at least 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns, making them ideal for applications in pharmaceuticals and electronics. ULPA filters, more stringent, filter out 99.999% of particles as small as 0.12 microns, suitable for critical environments like semiconductor manufacturing. Both are essential for maintaining contamination-free atmospheres in cleanrooms.</p></p>
