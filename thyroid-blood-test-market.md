<p><strong>The Thyroid Blood Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone (TSH)</li><li>Thyroxine (T4)</li><li>Triiodothyronine (T3)</li><li>Thyroglobulin (Tg)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thyroid blood test market includes key types that assess thyroid function and health. Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone (TSH) tests evaluate pituitary gland activity, indicating overall thyroid status. Thyroxine (T4) tests measure the primary hormone produced by the thyroid, essential for metabolism. Triiodothyronine (T3) tests assess the more active thyroid hormone, crucial for energy regulation. Thyroglobulin (Tg) tests are utilized mainly in monitoring thyroid cancer and can indicate residual disease. Each type plays a vital role in diagnosing and managing thyroid-related conditions.</p></p>
