<p><strong>The Atomic Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold atomic magnetometry</li><li>Spin-exchange relaxation-free (SERF) magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomic magnetometer market includes two main types: cold atomic magnetometers and spin-exchange relaxation-free (SERF) magnetometers. Cold atomic magnetometers use low-temperature atomic gases to enhance sensitivity and precision, making them suitable for applications in geophysics and biomedical imaging. SERF magnetometers leverage spin-exchange interactions in dense alkali vapor to achieve exceptionally high sensitivity and rapid response times, ideal for applications in navigation, security, and environmental monitoring. Both technologies are pivotal in advancing magnetometry capabilities across various industries.</p></p>
