<p><p>The satellite launch vehicle market is categorized into partially reusable and fully reusable types. Partially reusable vehicles utilize certain components, such as boosters or first stages, that can be recovered and refurbished for multiple flights, reducing costs. In contrast, fully reusable vehicles are designed to be launched, landed, and refurbished multiple times with minimal maintenance, significantly enhancing efficiency and affordability. Both types play a crucial role in increasing accessibility to space while driving innovation in launch technologies.</p></p>
