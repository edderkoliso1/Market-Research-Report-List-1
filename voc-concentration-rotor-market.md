<p><p>The VOC Concentration Rotor Market primarily consists of two main types: zeolite and activated carbon. Zeolite rotors use a crystalline structure to adsorb volatile organic compounds (VOCs) effectively, offering high adsorption capacity and selectivity. Activated carbon rotors, on the other hand, utilize porous carbon material to trap VOCs, providing a large surface area for adsorption. Both types are crucial for air purification and industrial applications, significantly improving air quality by efficiently concentrating and removing harmful VOCs from exhaust streams.</p></p>
