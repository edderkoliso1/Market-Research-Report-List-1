<p><p>The medical diagnostic imaging equipment market includes various device types essential for visualizing the internal structures of the body. X-ray devices utilize radiation to create images of bones and tissues for quick diagnosis. Ultrasound devices use sound waves to produce real-time images, often during pregnancy or for examining soft tissues. MRI employs magnetic fields and radio waves for detailed scans, while CT combines X-ray technology and computer processing for cross-sectional images. Other devices encompass advanced imaging modalities like PET and fluoroscopy.</p></p>
