<p><strong>The Congenital Heart Defect Closure Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The congenital heart defect closure devices market consists of several key types based on the specific conditions they address. Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) devices focus on closing holes between heart atria, while Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD) devices target openings in heart ventricles. Patent Foramen Ovale (PFO) closure devices are designed to seal the persistent openings between atria present since birth. Additionally, the "Others" category includes devices for various less common congenital heart defects requiring closure interventions, enhancing overall cardiac health.</p></p>
