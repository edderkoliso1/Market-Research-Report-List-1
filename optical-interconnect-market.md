<p><p>The optical interconnect market encompasses various technologies aimed at enhancing data transmission. Optical engines and transceivers convert electrical signals to optical signals and vice versa. Free space optics employ light beams in open environments for communication, while fibers and waveguides direct light through specific mediums, enabling fast and high-capacity data transfer in diverse applications.</p></p>
