<p><p>The Military Ground Robotic Vehicle market encompasses two primary types: Remote Controlled Vehicles (RCVs) and Unmanned Ground Vehicles (UGVs). RCVs are controlled by human operators from a distance, providing real-time observation and tactical support, often deployed for reconnaissance and explosive ordnance disposal. UGVs, on the other hand, operate autonomously or semi-autonomously, executing missions such as surveillance, logistics, and combat support without direct human control. Both types enhance operational efficiency and safety in military operations.</p></p>
