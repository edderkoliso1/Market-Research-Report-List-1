<p><p>The Hemodialysis Vascular Grafts market consists of several material types. Polyester grafts are commonly used due to their strength and durability. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) grafts offer excellent biocompatibility and resistance, making them ideal for long-term use. Polyurethane grafts provide flexibility and reduced thrombogenicity, enhancing patient comfort.
